About

As an HCI graduate, I've witnessed firsthand how technology can either empower or alienate users. Today's rapid AI advancement brings unprecedented opportunities, but also concerns that demand immediate attention: algorithmic bias excluding entire communities, opaque systems making life-altering decisions without explanation, and automation potentially displacing workers without providing pathways for adaptation.

This is precisely why now is the critical time to address these challenges. We're still in the early stages where thoughtful intervention can shape AI's trajectory toward human benefit. At Egg Labs, we apply human-centered design principles to ensure AI systems are transparent, inclusive, and genuinely supportive of human flourishing rather than mere technological advancement.

Research Focus

We are committed to advancing AI research in areas that promote safety, transparency, and beneficial outcomes for society. Our work emphasizes responsible development practices and ethical considerations in artificial intelligence.
